Biography

I am a PhD student in the MIT-WHOI Joint Program studying Applied Ocean Science and Engineering. I’m pursuing the development of a multi-robot system that can autonomously explore and build semantics maps of environments where communication is limited, such as the oceans. My co-supervisors are Dr. Yogesh Girdhar (WHOI) and Dr. Jonathan How (MIT).
